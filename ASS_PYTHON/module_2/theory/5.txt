1. Accessing tuple elements using positive and negative indexing. 

    ex : print(tuple[0])
         print(tuple[2])
         print(tuple[-1])
         print(tuple[-3])


2. Slicing a tuple to access ranges of elements. 

    ex : print(tuple[1:4])
         print(tuple[:3])
         print(tuple[2:])
         print(tuple[::2])
         print(tuple[::-1])
